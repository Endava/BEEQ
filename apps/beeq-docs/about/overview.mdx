BEEQ is created and maintained by a dedicated team of designers and developers from Endava, united by a shared commitment to building consistent, efficient, and accessible digital experiences.

## BEEQ Core Team and Contributors

The BEEQ Team is responsible for creating and maintaining the design system. It consists of designers and developers from Endava dedicated to ensuring consistency, efficiency, and usability across the design system.

The team works in the open. All component development, bug fixes, and design decisions happen on [GitHub](https://github.com/Endava/BEEQ), where anyone can follow progress, raise issues, and contribute.

## Contribution Guidelines

We welcome contributions from across the organisation to help improve and evolve BEEQ. Whether you are a designer, developer, or another kind of contributor, there are several ways to get involved:

- **Submit feedback** — If you encounter any issues or have suggestions for improvement, let us know via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Endava/BEEQ/issues) or by using the feedback button on any page.
- **Propose changes** — If you have ideas for new components or enhancements to existing ones, you can submit proposals for consideration.
- **Contribute code** — Developers can contribute to the codebase by fixing bugs, optimising performance, or adding new features. Make sure to read the [Contribution Guidelines](https://github.com/Endava/BEEQ/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) on GitHub before getting started.

<Tip>
  If you have any questions, feedback, or concerns, reach out to the BEEQ Team at [beeq@endava.com](mailto:beeq@endava.com). We look forward to collaborating with you to build exceptional digital experiences.
</Tip>
